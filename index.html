NOSXTINI

2 parts raspberry vodka*
1 part Chambord
1 part Triple sec
½ part lemonade

Fill a cocktail shaker with ice. Add vodka, Chambord, Triple sec, and lemonade. Shake. Serve up. Garnish with a lemon twist and head to the Lido Deck!

* If you don't have freshly infused raspberry vodka, store-bought is fine.
